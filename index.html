About me

My path started in fine arts—learning to see balance, texture, and mood. Interiors turned out to be where that mattered most: changing how a room feels changes how you live in it. I still find real joy in helping people shape spaces that feel like them.

When we work together, your priorities steer the project. I’m here for the careful, practical side of design too—so the finished space isn’t just beautiful, but livable.
